Who we are

The AWS Delhi/NCR Community is a platform that brings together AWS professionals, customers, partners, and enthusiasts in the Delhi/NCR region. Its mission is to foster collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and growth within the Amazon Web Services ecosystem. This community serves as a vibrant hub for individuals with a shared interest in AWS technologies. The primary objectives of the AWS Delhi/NCR Community include knowledge sharing, networking, and providing continuous learning opportunities. Members are encouraged to share their experiences, best practices, and real-world insights related to AWS services. Networking opportunities enable community members to connect, collaborate on projects, and build valuable professional relationships.

The community regularly organizes events, webinars, and workshops to enhance understanding of AWS services, showcase real-world use cases, and stay updated on the latest cloud computing trends. For #awsenthusiasts, this community offers a chance to contribute as speakers, write blogs to enrich the knowledge base, and participate in a dynamic, supportive environment for personal and professional growth. Join the AWS Delhi/NCR Community to be a part of this dynamic and collaborative space, where AWS enthusiasts can learn, share, and grow together.
